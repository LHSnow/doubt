Regler

Turneringsstil

Giants får köpas in! Alla vet väl att jättar bor i karga landskap där de kan äta sälar och renar när de vill. De får dessutom vara ensamma. Nu har de dock varit ensamma för länge och de vill träffa folk och leka! Det betyder att de har smugit sig in på varje roster för denna turnering.
En Giant från death zone räknas med i varje lags roster och kan således vara spelare nr 11 för priset av 300.000 (ja något billigare än priset i Death Zone)

Resurrection, 6 rundor swiss. Högst poäng efter match 6 vinner.

Resurrection tournament: inga SPP kan fås och eventuella skador eller dödsfall förs inte över till nästa match.

Alla lag räknas ha samma TV vid matchstart.

Regelförtydliganden

Turneringen använder Blood Bowl 2020 edition exhibition play rules, NAF 2021 rules for tournaments, GW Teams of legends (om det kommer ett nytt officiellt Khorne-roster i ett Spike Magazine senast 14 dagar innan turneringen så gäller det) och GW faq/errata.

För Kick-Off event Prayers for Nuffle så används tournament table (BB2020 Rulebook sida 103).

Eventuella spelare adderade till ett lags roster som Masters of Undeath eller Plague Ridden raderas efter matchen och följer således inte med till nästa match.

Learning by doing

Som DOUBT tidigare erbjudit så erhåller lagen skills efter lottningen inför match 4. Dessa erhålls permanent. Samma regler för skills gäller som tidigare. Behöver du tackle för att kunna spöa Hakflem? Guard för att rycka skägget av dvärgarna? Sure Hands för att det inte ska kosta alla RR att plocka upp bollen? Här har du chansen att visa din taktik med omsorg.

Tier 1-3 får 2 primary skills efter match 3

Tier 4-5 får 1 primary + 1 secondary efter match 3

Tidsbegränsning

2 timmar och 15 minuter per match. Schack klocka/app uppmanas att ha med 67 minuter vardera. Dessa används bl a för att ställa upp lag vid kick offs samt gå på WC under match. Målet är att samtliga matcher ska hinna spelas klart. Turneringsledningen har rätt att avbryta match som är på väg att dra över tiden, alternativt skärpa tidsbegränsningen. Dyker en spelare inte upp till matchstart dras tiden från hens matchtid, dvs från de 67 minutrarna. Tar tiden slut får spelarens figurer endast ställa sig upp på hens turns.

Etikett

Det är inte tillåtet att lägga sig i en annan match, undantaget för att påpeka regelbrott.

Det är alltid tillåtet att poängtera för Marty33 att han är ett taktiskt geni under turneringen.

Coacher har alltid rätt att under matchen byta eller dela tärningar med sin motcoach.

Om en coach vill så kan hen välja att spela med regular weather table under turneringen. Detta behöver annonseras innan runda 1.

Custom Weather Table for all

2D6 slås av turneringsansvarige innan varje omgång

2
Northern Freeze
It is so cold on the pitch that some players are close to becoming frozen statues. Unfortunately, so are the barracks...
Roll a D6 for each player (on the field, in the reserves and recovered from KO) after a touchdown is scored or when a half ends. On a roll of 1, the player has frozen to the ground for this drive. Frozen players may be set up as normal but they can not move by themselves. They have TZ, may be thrown, pushed, blocked, make a block, stand up, but may not move voluntarily by themselves.
3
Frozen Pitch
The ice on the ground is crystal clear. Players have a hard time to control themselves on the frozen pitch.
After a player has used their last square of MA (this could be after making a block if this was the players last MA to block, they must not follow up but may, make the slip roll after) a player will slip one random free square, ignoring TZ, as if the player was pushed from his own last vacated square. A player may not slip into an occupied square, unless all 3 squares was occupied. Then that player will be pushed back using same rules. After the slip, the player may attempt to rush as normal.
4
Frozen Ball
The conditions have made the ball, and other projectiles, to a frozen, lump of ice. This means it’s actually easier to throw things! And to the joy of the fans, it’s more dangerous as well.
All players increase their PA characteristic by 1 (4+ becomes 3+, - becomes 6+ etc). Also, if the pass is not caught, including failed conversions of a deflection, an AV roll is made against the player failing the catch. This is applied to failed kick off catches, but not throw ins nor hand offs. Note that a player may throw the ball on an opposing player in an attempt to injury him. However a pass not caught by a teammate is still considered a turnover.
5
Red Cross Field Day
The local Red Cross personel has decided that this is the day to practise their skills in action. Now you can get more then just a fashioned beard trim and a nice haircut on the sideline! Unfortunatly, this means that the ref’s are kicking it back and enjoying the show - ”If they want to practise, let them!”
Both teams each recieve a wandering apothecary (or igor or mortuary assistant). Both teams also recieve a bribe.
6-8
Nice
A bit windy and a bit cold, perfect blood bowl weather in Umeå!
No modifiers.
9
Unclear Markings
What’s this? All snow makes the lines very difficult to see and some players seems to be doing anything else then what they should be doing.
The kicking team may choose to treat the line of scrimmage one row of squares further into the opposing teams half, or one row of squares back into their own half. The recieving team must adhere to this new temporary line of scrimmage when setting up. This new LOS counts towards calculating a possible touchback. Also, any number of players may be set up in either wide zone for both teams.
10
Skare [ska’rä]
The snow has a hardened top layer. Perfectly safe to move on. Or is it?
Whenever a player makes a rush, add a -2 modifier. Players failing to rush is placed prone, no armour roll, and NO turnover is caused unless the playes was holding the ball.
11
Heavy Snow
The snow is several feet deep, making it very difficult indeed to run up and punch someone!
When a player performs a blitz and has moved at least one square, and makes the block, their strength characteristic is reduced by 1 when comparing their strength against their target of the block.
12
Blizzard
Freezing conditions and heavy falls makes the players behave irrational. The snowfall blocks the players visibility and something that was considered a bad move yesterday doesn’t seem so bad now!
During Blizzard all players with Really Stupid or the Bone Head traits loses these traits, while all other players gain Bone Head.
